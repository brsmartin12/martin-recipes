Momma Martin's Meatloaf

A Dish That Tastes Like Home

Just the smell of this dish's topping bubbling in the oven brings me back to 5th grade in my hometown of Jonesborough, Tennessee. It is a quintessential home cooked meal that is easy to prepare and hearty. Serve sliced along side some veggies and a helping of mashed potatoes for a very filling, nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped green pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup of oatmeal
  • 1 4 oz can tomato sauce

Topping:

  • 4 tbsp white vinegar
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 4 tbsp ketchup

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Mix all the ingredients (other than the topping) together into a large bowl.
  3. Grease a 2 qt baking dish then pour ingredients into the dish.
  4. In another small bowl mix all of the topping ingredients, then add to the top of the meat loaf.
  5. Bake for 1 hour.
